README.TXT - Hangman Game

Purpose:
The purpose of the Hangman game is to guess a word or phrase by selecting letters. The player has a limited number of attempts to guess the correct word or phrase before the game ends.

Directions:

Start the game by selecting a category.
The computer will select a word or phrase from the chosen category.
The player must guess the word or phrase by selecting letters one at a time.
If the selected letter is present in the word or phrase, it will be revealed in its corresponding position(s).
If the selected letter is not present in the word or phrase, the player loses one of their attempts.
The game ends when the player correctly guesses the word or phrase or runs out of attempts.
